ABOUT

An application, that serves as a platform for movement of agricultural products from the farms directly to the consumers or retailers.

- CoFarmer

This mobile and web application provides privilege for both farmers and consumers or retailers to buy and sell the required farm products without the involvement of a middleman at its right profitable price.
